Wiki Like Bio on Scott Hoying

Scott Richard Hoying was born in Arlington, Texas on September 17, 1991, which makes him 25 years of age. He is 6ft 3in tall. Scott is a singer, musician, and lyricist who is the vocalist of an acapella group called Phentatonix. He has won two Grammy alongside his group mate. Scott is one of three founding members of famous Pentatonix group.

Scott has been performing from his tender age(since 8 years of age). Constant hunger and love for music have made Scott what we know of him now. After graduating from Martin High School (in 2010) which is located in his home town, Hoying went to the University of Sothern California. There he formed the Pentatonix group with Mitch Grassi and Kirstie Maldonado. Later two more members Avi Kaplan and Kevin Olusola were added to the group. The group participated and won 3rd season of “The Sing Off” competition which earned the group sum of $200,000 and a recording contract with Sony.

Scott Hoying is not just content with Pentatonix he and his partner Mitch Grassi run Vlog channel in youtube which they created in August of 2013.  Future Friends – Part One is the first album Scott and Mitch have released together.

Scott Hoying has earned impressive sum from his profession. He has a net worth of $8M.
