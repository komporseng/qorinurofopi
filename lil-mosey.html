Lil Mosey Facts

  • He initially started with his musical pursuits by remaking songs by other popular artists.
  • Lil Mosey released his debut track, Off White on SoundCloud and it instantly became very popular and has garnered more than 50,000 views.
  • He participated as a contestant on Coast 2 Coast Live Seattle All Ages Edition on November 13, 2016, and wrapped up his journey at the 4th spot.
  • In 2018, Lil Mosey released his first commercial single titled Pull Up and its video received more than 25 million views on YouTube during the initial 16 months of its release.
  • He released his debut studio album, Northsbest on October 19, 2018, via Interscope Records and the album comprised of songs such as Kamikaze/Kamakazi, Burberry Headband, Rarri, Pull Up, Noticed, Greet Her, Yoppa, and Boof Pack.
  • The album, Northsbest, was certified ‘Gold’ by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and was also ranked at number #16 in the Norwegian Albums (VG-lista) chart, at number #20 in the Canadian Albums (Billboard) chart, at number #26 in the Swedish Albums (Sverigetopplistan) chart, and at number #29 in the US Billboard 200 chart.
  • The song, Noticed from Northsbest became a major hit with ‘Platinum’ certification by RIAA and was placed at number #1 in the Sverigetopplistan chart, at number #37 in the Ultratop 100 chart, at number #38 in the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart, and at number #80 in the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
  • Lil Mosey joined YouTube on March 25, 2014, and his channel has accrued more than 150 million views on its videos.
  • His song, G Walk, was released in the year 2019 and it featured singer, songwriter, dancer, actor, and entrepreneur, Chris Brown.
  • His sound has often drawn comparisons with rapper and songwriter, Meek Mill.
  • Lil Mosey released the track titled Stuck in a Dream (featuring Gunna) in 2019 and the song was placed at number #39 in the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart and at number #50 in the Canadian Hot 100 chart.
  • In January 2020, Lil Mosey was featured in Eladio Carrión’s song Safe With Me.
  • In February 2020, he released a new song titled Blueberry Faygo, which debuted at No. 62 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
  • In March 2020, he was featured in Rvssian’s single Only The Team.
  • In June 2020, Lil Mosey was featured in The Kid LAROI‘s song WRONG. The track peaked at No. 9 on the US Bubbling Under the Hot 100 chart and No. 98 on the Canadian Hot 100 chart.
  • In April 2021, Lil Mosey was charged with one count of second-degree rape in Lewis County, Washington, after he was accused of engaging in s*xual intercourse with a person who was incapable of giving consent. He later pled “not guilty” to the charges. He was ordered not to come within 500 feet of the victim and was prohibited from possessing firearms or using drugs. In 2023, it was revealed that he had passed a polygraph test. The trial started on February 22, 2023, and on March 2, 2023, he was found not guilty.
  • In October 2022, he released a 3-track EP titled UNI.
  • In November 2022, Lil Mosey released a new EP titled VER.
