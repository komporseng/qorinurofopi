Who is Tracy McGrady:

Tracy McGrady is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on May 24, 1979 and his birthplace is Bartow, FL. Tracy is also well known as, 7-time NBA All-Star known as T-MAC who was one of the league’s top scorers while playing for the Orlando Magic and Houston Rockets. He led the NBA in scoring in 2002-03 and 2003-04.

Tracy is originated from United States. He was on a great Houston Rockets team alongside star Chinese center Yao Ming, but they could never get past the second round of the playoffs.
