Know About Calvin Ridley; Stats, Age, Brother, Salary, Height, Dating

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Like his younger brother, Calvin Ridley earns recognition for his professional stint as an American football wide receiver. He played college football at Alabama before announcing himself into the National Football League (NFL).

Calvin Ridley is associated with the NFL side, Atlanta Falcons. He made his way into the team via the 2018 NFL Draft.

Calvin Ridley Short Bio; Age, Brother, Height

The 24-years-of-age player was born as Calvin Orin Ridley on 20 December 1994 in Coconut Creek, Florida, the United States. Talking about his family, his younger brother, Riley Ridley, is an NFL wide receiver for the Chicago Bears.

About 6 feet and 1 inch (1.85 m) tall in height, Calvin attended Monarch High School in his hometown where he played high school football. Later on, he committed to the University of Alabama to play college football on 19 April 2014.

Career Stats, Salary

After a promising three-season stint at Alabama with 2,781 receiving yards and 19 touchdowns from 44 games, Calvin Ridley was drafted by the Atlanta Falcons in the first round of the 2018 NFL Draft. Eventually, he penned a four-year, $10.9 million deal with the team that also featured a $6.1 million signing bonus. As per the deal, he bags an average salary of $2,725,178.

Although he remained quiet in his NFL debut against the Philadelphia Eagles, Calvin landed four receptions for 64 yards and his first NFL touchdown in his side’s 31-24 triumph over the Carolina Panthers. Furthermore, he recorded seven receptions for 146 yards and three touchdowns in a 43-37 loss to the New Orleans Saints.

In week 2 of the 2019 season, he caught 8 passes for 105 yards and a touchdown as the Falcons defeated Philadelphia Eagles by 24-20 scoreline.
