Some Lesser Known Facts About Dalton Gomez

  • Dalton Gomez is an American real estate agent and fiance of the popular American singer Ariana Grande.
  • Dalton works at Aaron Kirman Group, a real estate agency in Los Angeles, and for 5 years (as of 2020), he has been working with the company. In addition to this, he worked as the Director of Operations for the first three years of his work in the company.
  • He is the A-list buyer in the company and has represented notable sales including Pierre Koenig’s Case Study House #21, Craig Ellwood’s Case Study House #1, and Oscar Niemeyer House (also known as Strick House) in North America (with Aaron Kirman), and has also represented table works by architects such as Richard Neutra, John Lautner, and A. Quincy Jones.
  • According to a publication, Dalton and Ariana came across each other when her team hired Aaron Kirman Group to help her find a house before the COVID-19 pandemic. Ariana demanded her team to plan an in-person meeting with Dalton because she found him attractive. Soon, they started seeing each other but kept their relationship private.

    Dalton Gomez with Ariana Grande

  • In February 2020, his relationship with Ariana came into notice when they were seen getting intimate with each other during a dinner with friends at Bar Louie in Northridge Fashion Center in Los Angeles.
  • In May 2020, Ariana confirmed her relationship with Dalton when she appeared alongside Dalton in the music video of the stay-at-home duet ‘Stuck with U,’ which she sang with Justin Bieber. Towards the end of the video, Dalton was visible dancing with Ariana while the couple showed their love for each other.
  • Before Dalton, Ariana has been in a relationship with the American rapper Big Sean from 2014 to 2015, her backup dancer Ricky Alvarez from 2015 to 2016, and the late American rapper Mac Miller from 2016 to 2018. From June 2018 to October 2018, she was engaged to the Saturday Night Live star Pete Davidson. There have been rumors about her being in a relationship with Social House member Mikey Foster.

He is the type of guy she has been looking for. He’s very protective of her, will go out of his way for her and does little things that are chivalrous. Dalton will go above and beyond to meet her needs.”
