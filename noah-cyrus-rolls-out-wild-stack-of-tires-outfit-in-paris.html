Noah Cyrus rolls out wild stack of tires look in Paris: Michelin man slay

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Noah Cyrus always makes a statement with her style, whether she’s showing skin or all covered up.

The “July” singer posted photos from Paris Thursday wearing yet another avant-garde outfit — this time a full-body black puffer gown by Colors, which features multiple zippers so the wearer can create different looks.

Cyrus, 23, chose to bundle up from head to toe in the rings, and her fans couldn’t help but poke fun at the unusual ensemble.

“It’s giving stack of tires, but in a slay way of course,” one person commented, while another asked, “Y u a caterpillar?”

Other hilarious comments included, “It’s giving air mattress,” “michelin man slay” and “Why this feel like that montage in the Lizzie Mcguire movie where she tried on the igloo.”

One person joked, “When you have dinner at 7 but have to go camping at 9,” and another referenced the viral TikTok question all girlfriends pose to their boyfriends: “Would you still love me if I was a worm.”

The internet celebrity known as Benny Drama approved of the statement-making look, commenting “Legend,” while mom Tish Cyrus dropped a few fire emojis.

Last week, the younger Cyrus sister went in the opposite direction, facing the freezing temperatures in France during Paris Fashion Week by freeing the nipple in a deeply plunging gown that featured a chain detail around the neckline.

And, after returning stateside, the singer kept the fashion party going at the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party in Los Angeles on Thursday, wearing a ruffled baby bonnet and sheer gloves as part of her all-white outfit.

With New York Fashion Week around the corner, there’s no telling how the star will top her recent looks.
