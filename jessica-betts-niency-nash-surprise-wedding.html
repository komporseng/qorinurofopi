Jessica Betts Got Married To Niecy Nash

May 2024 · 2 minute read

American singer Jessica Betts is a married woman now. She is married to actress Niecy Nash, leading a same-sex marriage. Actress Niecy Nash opened about her marriage through her social media platforms on 31 August 2020.

Nash shared a picture on her Instagram and Twitter that featured both of the stars studded in their wedding dress at the ceremony. 'Mrs. Carol Denise Betts' and the hashtag '#LoveWins,' she captioned the image.

Jessica Betts and Niecy Nash (©: Instagram/Niecy Nash)

Jessica Betts donned in a white wedding tuxedo while Niecy Nash wore a floral white long wedding gown as they walked down the aisle. But it's unclear where their surprise wedding took place.

Currently, the lovebirds are over the moon with their newly tied marital status. Congratulations to the couple.

Who Is Jessica Betts? Know About Her Parents

Jessica Betts is the daughter of the legendary musician Dickey Betts, who was the guitarist of the famous band, Allman Brothers. Her father and Allman Brothers were one of the notable bands of the classic rock history. Allman Brothers named their classic hit 'Jessica' on her name.

Similarly, her mother is Donna Betts.

Jessica, an American singer/songwriter, is currently signed with the Paid 4 Entertainment and Kita Brown Music. She is best known for her songs including Sleep in the Morning, Holdin On, Missin You More, Block, Love Is Special, I Need Luv, and many others. Her music is widely-influenced by Aretha Franklin, Sam Cooke, Whitney Houston, Kim Burrell, Tracy Chapman, and more.

She first gained her prominence after winning the first and only season of the reality competition series The Road to Stardom With Missy Elliott. 

Besides that, Jessica Betts appeared in the tv show named Claws back in 2018. The singer shared a video dated back to 2018, where Nash and she were seen “hanging out” on set as homegirls after she appeared in an episode of Claws, as the character Nadege.
