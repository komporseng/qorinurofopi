Da Brat &amp; Jesseca Harris-Dupart Share First Photos Of Their Newborn

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Da Brat and Jesseca Harris-Dupart have given fans a peak at their baby boy True Legend Harris-Dupart, sharing a heartwarming photoshoot — check it out below.

On Wednesday (August 9), both the former So So Def rapper and serial entrepreneur shared photos of their newborn in an exclusive spread with PEOPLE.

AD LOADING...

In each photo, the loving pair are tucked close with their baby boy, while dressed in gray loungewear. True Legend is matching his parents with a gray hat, white shirt, gray joggers, and white socks.

Da Brat spoke to the outlet about being a month into motherhood and how she feels about giving birth after having a high-risk pregnancy.

“I’m 49, I’m high-risk, I have high blood pressure,” she said. “So many women so much younger than me want kids and can’t have them, and I had a successful pregnancy. It’s just an honor. He’s such a blessing.”

AD LOADING...

She also spoke about receiving vitro fertility IVF treatments from her wife’s eggs and sperm from an anonymous donor to become pregnant, which took place after she had a miscarriage.

“I think God plans out our journey,” she said. “I’m very happy He saw fit for me to have him.”

Da Brat and Jesseca Harris-Dupart gave birth on July 6 in Atlanta. The Chicago rapper told the aforementioned outlet upon giving birth: “I can’t BELIEVE he came out of me! Feels like a dream. He’s PERFECT in every way.”

Harris-Dupart also gave a statement to the outlet saying: “Very appreciative for such a blessing of our bundle of joy. This journey has been more amazing than we could’ve ever imagined.”

AD LOADING...

Shortly after True Legend Harris-Dupart was born, Jesseca Harris-Dupart had to clarify that the name of their newborn son is simply a combination of her and Da Brat’s two names together and that they did not steal the names from anyone, despite a former employee’s claim.

Once Da Brat gave birth to their son, she announced that she and her wife would name him Legacy Shawntae Dupart at the time.

A former employee of Jesseca’s then accused the happy couple of stealing the names of her two children, but Jesseca bit back on Instagram by resharing a 2020 interview Da Brat did with Dish Nation, where she said the names were a combination of her and Jesseca’s.

“Shawntae is my first name and Dupart is my baby’s last name,” Brat said at the time. “Carry on the legacy of the parents, darling.”
