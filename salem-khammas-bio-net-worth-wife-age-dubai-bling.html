Salem Khammas On Dubai Bling

How Much Is Salem Khammas Net Worth?

Salem Khammas’s net worth is above $50 million.

In the United Arab Emirates, Salem serves as the chairman of four construction companies: Abraj Constructions, Autocad Construction, Kunoz Engr Consultancy, and Shamal GT, according to his LinkedIn. After starting his own company, Shamal General Trading, in 2016, the businessman began working for Autocad Construction in 2018. He joined Kunoz Engr. in the same month that he founded the business.

Salem had taken over as chairman of Abraj Constructions just three months prior. In July 2021, his company secured the “largest project with the Ministry of Energy and Infrastructure in the Emirate of Ras Al Khaimah.”

Due to his achievements, CEO and entrepreneur Salem is thought to have a millionaire net worth similar to that of his co-stars, having worked for billionaires like Naveen Jindal. Naveen is an Indian billionaire industrialist, philanthropist, and a former Member of Parliament.

Because of his work as a chairman and entrepreneur, Salem travels the world, visiting places like Paris and Japan. He served as executive director for Al Ahli, a renowned aluminum, glass, and cladding contractor in the United Arab Emirates. He currently serves as chairman of four businesses, one of which he owns.

Salem’s career began as a bank manager at Abu Dhabi Commercial Bank in 2002. He quit his job there in 2007. He was also an executive director at Al Ahli Holding Group for 9 years from 2007 to 2015.

Besides his job, Salem is also into horse beauty competitions. “All thanks and appreciation to the technical staff of the Fujairah Arabian Horse Championship. Big thanks to the organizing team of Fujairah Arabian horse beauty,” Salem posted on his IG.

Salem graduated from California State University with a bachelor’s degree in accounting. He earned an MBA from Zayed University.
