Who Is Sky Brown? Meet The Skateboarder And Know About Her Parents And Family

July 2024 · 2 minute read

It's good for children to know about their future careers instead of being in a dilemma. As age does not matter in any of the things that prevail in this world, success is also roofed under it. And Sky Brown can be one of the prominent examples of this.

Sky Brown is just eight years old, and yet she has already summoned the world's attention towards her. She is a pro-skateboarder, and it's surprising to find a child of such age being at the world-class level of athleticism.

Let's dig some more information on Sky Brown below!!

Who Is Sky Brown?

To those who may not know about Sky Brown, she is the Japanese professional skateboarder. Concerning her professional life, she was the youngest sportsperson to participate in the 2016 Vans US Open Pro Series.

Likewise, in 2017, she attended the first-ever Asian Vans Park Series Continental Championship in Singapore.

Sky Brown in the 2016 Vans US Open Pro Series (©: Hypebeast)

Later in 2018, she was the part of 'Starting Lineup' of the Teck Deck skateboarding brand. In the same year, Brown went to Dancing With The Stars and finished in second place in 2018. During the season, Alan Bersten helped her as a mentor.

Her first qualifying game for the Tokyo Olympics 2020 was in the Dew Tour 2019 in Long Beach, California, in the United States. She showcased her excellence in the games, even with her broken arms. Sky Brown was undefeated in the 'Under 16' group. As a skateboarder, she is proudly presented by Abrahms Artists Agency and Select Management Group.

Besides that, Sky Brown appeared in the 2017 tv series Little Big Shots, and her social value has immensely increased. She has 594k followers on her Instagram, as of now.

More About Sky Brown: Her Parents And Family

Born in Miyazaki, Japan, in 2008, Sky Brown celebrates her birthday on 7 July. She is the oldest daughter of her parents, Stuart Brown and Mieko Brown. Her mom and dad raised her along with her sibling named Ocean Brown in her family, who is also a skateboarder.

The 12-year-old pro sportsperson stands at the height of 4 feet and 6 inches tall and weighs 37kg in weight.

Besides that, Sky Brown has double nationality, both of Great Britain and Japan. She has represented both Japan and Great Britain as a skateboarder. She is of mixed ethnicity as her mom is Japanese, and her dad is British.
