DD Osama Net worth: How Old is the Harlem Drill Rapper?

May 2024 · 5 minute read

DD Osama is a rising rap star from Harlem, New York, who has gained popularity for his drill music and his viral songs such as “Without You”, “Dead Opps”, and “40s N 9s”.

He is also the brother of the late Notti Osama, who was also a rapper and his frequent collaborator.

In this article, we will explore some facts about DD Osama, such as his age, real name, height, girlfriend, net worth, parents, songs, and death.

DD Osama Age

DD Osama was born on November 29, 2006, in Harlem, New York.

As of 2023, he is 16 years old.

He started rapping in 2021 and released his first song “Aftermath” with his brother Notti Osama and another rapper named Blockwork.

DD Osama began to receive more attention in 2022, after releasing his hit songs “Without You” and “40s N 9s”, which have over 10 million views on YouTube.

He also appeared as a guest artist on the song “PARTY IN THE USA” in 2022.

DD Osama Real Name

DD Osama’s real name is David DeShaun Reyes.

As a tribute to his father, who shares the same name, he chose DD Osama as his stage name.

He sometimes goes by the nickname D. Reyes or DD.

How Tall is DD Osama?

DD Osama is 5 feet 7 inches (170 cm) tall and weighs around 125.5 lbs (57 kg). He has a slim build and black hair and eyes. He often wears hoodies, caps, and chains as part of his rap style.

DD Osama Girlfriend

DD Osama Net Worth

DD Osama Parents
