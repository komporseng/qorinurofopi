Who is Terrence Howard?

Terrence Howard is an American actor and singer.

Terrence Howard is extremely prominent for the film ‘The Best Man’ (1999), ‘Crash’, ‘Ray’ (2004), ‘Hustle & Flow’ (2005), and T.V series ‘Empire’ (2015-present), ‘Wayward Pines’ (2015-2016), etc.

He  is a hardworking person and has made a recognizable position in the American entertainment field.

Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Howard was born in Tyrone and Anita Howard on March 11, 1969, in Chicago, Illinois, U.S.A. His nationality is American and belongs to the African-American ethnicity.

His mother’s name is Anita Hawkins Williams and his father’s name is Tyrone Howard. He was raised with his father in a rocky childhood and disciplinary environment.

Howard’s father stabbed another man when Terrance was only two years old for which he spent months in Jail. His parents also divorced when his father was released from jail.

Terrence Howard: Professional Life, Career, Awards

Terrence Howard started his acting career in the year 1992 and appeared in the film “The Jacksons: An American Dream” as “Jackie Jackson”.

Though he appeared in that film in a minor role, he didn’t get more reviews. He also appeared in other low-rated movies in the initial phase of his career including “Mr. Holland’s Opus”, “Sunset Park”, “Butter”, “The Players Club”, etc.

In the year 1999, he appeared in a film named “The Best Man” as ‘Quentin Spivey’ for which he won ‘NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Motion Picture’ and was nominated for more than three awards.

One of his successful films in his lifetime career was “Crash” as ‘Cameron Thayer’ for which he won 7 awards including ‘Black Reel Award for Best Supporting Actor, ‘Florida Film Critics Circle Pauline Kael Breakout Award’, ‘Vancouver Film Critics Circle Award for Best Supporting Actor and nominated for more than five awards.

Terrence Howard made his debut on T.V in the year 1994 and appeared in one episode of “Family Matters”. One of his successful T.V series is “Empire” (2015-present) for which he won ‘The BET Award for Best Actor and was nominated for more than five awards.

Howard declared his reirement from acting in Sepember 2019 because he was tired of pretending. On the other hand, he was confirmed to star in the British sci-fi thriller movie Beneath on February 1, 2021.

Also, he was cast in the horror film Skeletons in the Closet in February 1, 2022.
